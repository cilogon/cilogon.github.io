Where did QDL come from?

QDL (Quick and Dirty Language) originated as a server-side scripting tool for Open Authorization servers.

Philosophy

The basic philosophies are

* Small is beautiful * Aim at probabilities (what you are likely to do) vs. possibilities (edge cases) * Simple things should be simple
